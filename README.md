## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **US FMCG Sales Dashboard** designed to analyze sales performance, revenue contribution, discount behavior, regional trends, and sales representative efficiency across the United States FMCG market.

The dashboard provides a high-level business overview along with category-wise, regional, and city-level performance insights to support strategic business decisions.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall FMCG sales performance
- Monitor revenue contribution by product category and sales channel
- Evaluate discount impact on revenue
- Identify top-performing regions, cities, and sales representatives
- Understand category-wise discount patterns
- Support data-driven sales and marketing decisions

# 📈 Dashboard Insights

## 1️⃣ Revenue by Product Category & Channel

The dashboard compares revenue generated through **Retail** and **Wholesale** channels across major FMCG categories.

| Category | Retail Revenue | Wholesale Revenue |
|----------|----------------|------------------|
| Household | 40K | 18K |
| Dairy | 35K | 23K |
| Beverages | 33K | 22K |
| Snacks | 32K | 20K |

### 🔍 Key Insights
- **Household** category generated the highest retail revenue (**40K**).
- Retail sales outperform wholesale sales across all categories.
- Dairy products have the strongest wholesale contribution (**23K**).
- Product categories show balanced revenue distribution, indicating diversified sales.
